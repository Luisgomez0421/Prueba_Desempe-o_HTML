About the Band

Shattered Riffs is more than just a rock band — it's an experience. Known for explosive energy and electrifying live shows, we deliver a powerful fusion of raw sound, emotion, and attitude. Every riff, every beat, and every lyric is crafted to ignite the crowd and create moments that stay long after the music stops.

Our Influences

  • Timeless Classic Rock that defined the spirit of rebellion
  • Heavy Hard Rock driven by aggressive riffs and bold attitude
  • Innovative Alternative sounds that break the rules and evolve the genre

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We stay real — no filters, no compromises
  • Intensity: Every performance is delivered with full force and passion
  • Connection: We turn music into a shared experience with our audience
